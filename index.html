About Us

She Can Foundation is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in India, registered under the Indian Society Registration Act, 1860. It focuses on empowering marginalized communities through education, livelihood development, and social inclusion initiatives. The organization emphasizes gender equality, youth empowerment, and community-driven sustainable development.

Mission & Vision

The foundation’s mission is to create equitable opportunities that enable individuals—especially women and youth—to achieve economic independence and personal growth. Its vision is a socially inclusive society where marginalized groups actively contribute to community and national development.

Program & Initiatives

She Can Foundation runs skill development workshops, educational outreach programs, and community awareness campaigns. These initiatives aim to reduce socio-economic disparities by improving access to education, vocational training, and employment opportunities.

Governance and Approach

The NGO follows a participatory approach, involving community members in decision-making and program design. Transparency and sustainability are key principles in its governance, ensuring that each initiative is locally relevant and long-lasting.

Our Registration Certificate

She Can Foundation is officially registered under the Indian Society Act, 1860. This certification reflects our commitment towards empowering women, promoting education, leadership development, and creating equal opportunities for all.

Our foundation continuously works to support communities through impactful social initiatives, awareness campaigns, and volunteer-driven programs focused on women empowerment and youth development.
